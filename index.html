Conditional: The Final Execution

Episode 1: If It Happens, It Happens

By Shrushty Vandre • July 12, 2025

🌧️ It was a stormy night. Rain lashed the windows. Somewhere in the city, a line of code was about to change everything…

In a dimly lit room, Shrushty sat hunched over her terminal. Her hoodie cast a shadow across her determined eyes. The screen glowed as she whispered:

"This isn't just code... it's a trigger."

The script blinked:

if clue == "decoded":
   print("Proceed to Sector 7")

Suddenly, a voice echoed from the speaker:

“Condition met. Access granted.”

A wall in front of her slid open, revealing a biometric scanner. She stepped forward, heart racing. Swiped her hand.

🟥 BEEP — ACCESS DENIED

“Why didn’t it work? The condition was true.”

She pulled out her old black notebook: “THE CONDITIONALS”. Scribbled inside were messy words:

“Not all truths are the same in the world of logic.”

Her eyes widened. “Case-sensitive…” she muttered, smirking. She updated the code:

if clue == "Decoded":

Still, not good enough. She tried again — smarter this time:

if clue.lower() == "decoded":
   print("Proceed to Sector 7")

🟩 BEEP — ACCESS GRANTED

Behind the wall: a secret vault. Massive screens flickered alive. Lines of code scrolled endlessly. The terminal lit up with one final message:

WELCOME, SHRUSHTY.
NEXT CHALLENGE: "ELSE IF YOU DARE…"

She smiled. Ready for whatever came next.
